void ModuleDenyChannels::OnCheckJoin ( ChannelPermissionData &  join) [inline, virtual]

Called whenever a user is about to join a channel. This hook is called prior to the checks for channel ban, limit, invite, etc; the normal OnPermissionCheck hook is called after those checks.

Change the result of the join to MOD_RES_DENY to forbid the join, or to MOD_RES_ALLOW to skip further checks.
